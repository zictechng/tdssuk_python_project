Privacy Policy

At TDSSUK Global Services Ltd, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide when using our website, contacting our team, or utilizing our logistics, cargo, freight forwarding, shipping, customs clearance, and delivery services.

By accessing our website or using our services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and business information necessary to provide our services effectively. The information collected may include:

  • Full name and contact details.
  • Email address and telephone number.
  • Business or company information.
  • Billing and payment information.
  • Shipping, sender, and recipient details.
  • Identification and customs documentation where required.
  • Website usage and communication records.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, improve, and manage our logistics services while maintaining compliance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

  • Process and manage shipping requests.
  • Coordinate cargo transportation and delivery.
  • Facilitate customs clearance procedures.
  • Respond to enquiries and customer support requests.
  • Issue invoices and process payments.
  • Provide shipment updates and service notifications.
  • Improve our services and customer experience.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share relevant information with trusted service providers, logistics partners, customs authorities, government agencies, and payment processors where necessary to perform our services or comply with legal requirements.

Any third-party access to personal information is limited to what is necessary for the performance of their responsibilities and is subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, misuse, or destruction. While we strive to maintain the highest security standards, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, improve website functionality, and analyze visitor interactions. Cookies help us understand how visitors use our website and enable us to provide a more efficient browsing experience.

  • Improve website performance and functionality.
  • Remember user preferences.
  • Analyze website traffic and visitor behavior.
  • Enhance security and fraud prevention measures.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, including operational, legal, accounting, tax, compliance, and record-keeping requirements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on applicable data protection laws, you may have the right to request access to your personal information, request corrections, object to certain processing activities, request data deletion where appropriate, and withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

Requests relating to personal data may be submitted through our official contact channels, and we will respond in accordance with applicable legal requirements.

8. International Data Transfers

As part of providing international logistics and shipping services, your information may be transferred to countries outside your jurisdiction. Where such transfers occur, we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are implemented to protect your personal information.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of external websites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites they visit.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

TDSSUK Global Services Ltd reserves the right to update, modify, or revise this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will become effective upon publication on our website. We encourage users to review this page periodically to remain informed about how we protect personal information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, your personal information, or our data handling practices, please contact our customer support team. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
