Who We Are And What We Do.

TDSS UK was established in the United Kingdom to meet up to the expectations of businessmen, exporters and importers who actively or intend to run logistics to West African countries.
We have always offered a wide range of worldwide shipping services.
From the onset we took pride in customer service & slowly began to build a base for the business bringing in new customers whilst providing repeat work for existing customers.


TDSS UK actively ships to Nigeria and Ghana but based on huge demand in shipping goods, we have decided to extend our services to all West African countries.
After many years of building our business relationships with overseas clearing agents. We were soon shipping cars, vans, trucks buses & containers to the region.
